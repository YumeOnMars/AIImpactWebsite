The Problem

Training and Running AI models requires a ton of energy. According to MIT News, the training process for OpenAI GPT-3 consumed 1,287 megawatt hours (MWh) of electricity.

Equivalent to...

The amount of electricity able to power 120 average U.S homes for a year

Releasing 552 Tons of Carbon Dioxide into the atmosphere

Training and running AI models use a lot of water to keep computers cool. This can take water away from local water supplies and disrupt econsystems nearby.
